nos.social nostr relay

Relay.nos.social plays a pivotal role in maintaining a healthy digital environment on nos.social. Leveraging the capabilities of the Reportinator bot, user notes are analyzed for inappropriate or offensive content using OpenAI's moderation API. Notes identified as such are published directly to our relay as kind 1984, ensuring immediate storage and handling. In our app, these flagged notes are distinctively rendered, requiring users to actively choose to view them, in support of a healthy and constructive online discourse. This process aligns with NIP-56, underlining our dedication to user safety and content integrity.

To use this relay, add wss://relay.nos.social to your client relays on clients that handle moderation reports like the nos.social app.
